关于我们About Us

项目介绍

上海漫步是一个致力于展示和传播上海本土文化的项目。我们希望通过这个平台，让更多人了解上海的历史文化遗产，感受这座城市的独特魅力。

上海弄堂文化

弄堂（longtang）是上海最具特色的城市肌理，它不仅是一种建筑形式，更是承载着上海人记忆的生活场所。在这里，我们可以看到：

  • 石库门建筑的独特魅力
  • 邻里之间的温情互动
  • 地道的上海市井生活
  • 传统与现代的完美融合

Project Introduction

ShanghaiWalk is a project dedicated to showcasing and spreading Shanghai's local culture. Through this platform, we aim to help more people understand Shanghai's historical and cultural heritage, experiencing the unique charm of this city.

Shanghai Lane (Longtang) Culture

The lanes (longtang) are Shanghai's most distinctive urban fabric, representing not just an architectural form but also a living space that carries the memories of Shanghai people. Here, we can see:

  • The unique charm of Shikumen architecture
  • Warm interactions between neighbors
  • Authentic Shanghai community life
  • Perfect fusion of tradition and modernity

  • 独特的海派文化
  • 地道的上海方言
  • 传统的沪剧艺术
  • 特色的江南美食
  • 丰富的民间习俗
